

OSSIFICATION CENTERS

An ossification center is the place where the ossification begins in the cartilaginous draft of the bone.

    -Primary ossification center: beginning of the ossification within the cartilaginous draft. For emeple: the diaphysis of the long bones. The epiphysis remain cartilaginous.
    
    -Secondary ossification center: appears after the primary ossification center, for exemple: the epiphysis of the long bones.
