

ADDUCTOR TUBERCLE (OF FEMUR)


The adductor tubercle is a tubercle on the lower extremity of the femur. 

It is formed where the medial lips of the linea aspera end below at the summit of the medial condyle. 

It is the insertion point of the tendon of the vertical fibers of the adductor magnus muscle.
