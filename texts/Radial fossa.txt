

RADIAL FOSSA


The radial fossa is a slight depression found on the humerus above the front part of the capitulum. 

It receives the anterior border of the head of the radius when the forearm is flexed.


== Structure ==

The joint capsule of the elbow attaches to the humerus just proximal to the radial fossa.
