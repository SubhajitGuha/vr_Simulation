

CLAVICULAR FACET OF SCAPULA


Clavicular facet of scapula is small oval facet on the medial border of the acromion for articulation with the acromial facet on the lateral end of the clavicle. 

Also called Clavicular articular facet of acromion.


The coracoacromial ligament is attached near the clavicular facet.
